Description: In Cairo many farmers transport their animals in boxes on top of their cars. Therefore the goal is to share these boxes with the stray dogs. In the city the population is very poor and may not afford much to help stray dogs. These boxes are cheep and the concept exists already in Cairo for other species. Therefore the farmers can just learn the dogs to get inside the boxes and they will have a save space.
